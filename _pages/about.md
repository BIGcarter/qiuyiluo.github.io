Hello, welcome!

I am Qiuyi Luo (罗秋怡), a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Tokyo.
My research focuses on star formation, with an emphasis on the formation of multiple stellar systems within the Milky Way. I utilize radio interferometric and single-dish observations from facilities such as ALMA, JCMT, and the NRO 45-m telescope to investigate how multiplicity evolves across various Galactic environments.
